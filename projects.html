860MW Baynes HPP, Namibia/Angola

This project on the Kunene River consists of a large RCC arch main dam generating peaking energy of around 860MW. An 18MW Balancing Dam is situated downstream to re-regulate the flows back to a constant flow for environmental and social purposes. This Technical Advisory Service is being provided through SMEC for the PJTC of Angola and Namibia.

The Himba people are the traditional land owners
