About

Brian has been involved in the below projects as Owner’s Engineer, Owner’s Designer, Contractor’s Designer, Lender’s Technical Advisor and Independent Engineer.

Experienced in the following stages of work:

- Strategic planning and concept studies
- Pre-feasibility and feasibility studies
- Tender and FEED Design
- Detailed Design
- Pre-qualification process
- Tender documents and tender process:
   o Instruction to tenderers (and request for bid instructions)
   o Employer’s Requirements or specifications
   o Reference design and design basis memorandum
   o Geological Baseline Report
   o Environmental, social and safety management and                    monitoring plans
   o Reference documents and drawings
   o Scoring mechanisms
   o Award negotiations and pre-contract clarification meetings
- Contract and site supervision
- Commissioning, testing and troubleshooting
- Upgrade, refurbishment and retrofitting hydropower 

Qualifications and Memberships

– Chartered Engineer (CEng), Engineering Council UK, 1993
– Member of the Institution of Civil Engineers (MICE), 1993
– Former member of the Chartered Institute of Water and Environmental Managers (MCIWEM), 1993-2017
– MSc Water Resources Technology, University of Birmingham, 1988
– BSc (Hons) Civil Engineering, University of Nottingham, 1987
